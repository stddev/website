About us

Standard Dies and Tools, founded in 1980, is an industry leader in the manufacturing of Tungsten Carbide MIG wire dies.

Our manufacturing facilities boast state-of-the-art equipment for production and verification of highest quality and most consistent Tungsten Carbide wire drawing dies. Our manufacturing flexibility combined with our large and diverse inventory allows us to offer short run, high volume, customized and standard products delivered with the shortest lead times in the industry.

Our capabilities include operations such as Turning, Precision ID Grinding and Center less OD Grinding, Lapping, Polishing, and Finishing.

Our thirty plus years of field experience enables us to provide utmost satisfaction to the customer by providing unsurpassed quality for all the dies.

Since our inception more than 30 years ago, we have let quality be our single guiding principle. Our policy and goal is to do it right the first time and to continually supply tools and materials that always exceed customer's expectation. Our client feedback is our biggest marketing tool.

Our technical expertise is available to all of our customers in all the phases of the production.
